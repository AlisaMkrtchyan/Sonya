About us

Hairbands by Sonya was set up in 2022, after Sonya was asked to design a friends bridal headpiece. She enjoyed every minute of creating it and the bride loved it. She received a few enquiries from guests at her wedding…… et voila….. Hairbands by Sonya was created.

Who we are?

She create the pearl and silk headbands personally and the leather headbands and fascinators are designed and finished off by her and she has her crafts people make them. The leathers and suedes she use are all of top quality and the quality of craftsmanship is second to none.

Let’s see more About us and our shop

Sonya has recently introduced matching bouquets to match the headpiece. And other accessories to compliment handbags etc. You can see the full collection on sonyawmcleod2 and HairbandsbySonya. I try to make everyone unique in some way, the colours of materials, the decorations added etc.

Bespoke orders are welcome, The headbands and fascinators compliment brides dresses, bridesmaids and guests outfits, special events etc .The headbands can be worn casually or for any event. Please allow three weeks from date of order to date of delivery, if I she send sooner, she will.

She is based in Armenia and will use standard registered postal service to send to your address. If you are interested in working in a partnership – stocking her designs, please do not hesitate to contact her directly.
